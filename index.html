About Me

My name is Landon Lynch. I am a rising Junior at Arsenal Tech High School, and I live in Indianapolis, Indiana. I’ve been interested in computers since I got my first laptop at 7 years old, but I hadn’t realized the impact that technology has on our society until I was about 13. Computer science has single-handedly changed my outview on the world, who I am, and the path I’ve ventured on in my life. Before looking into positions in computer science I didn’t realize the amount of change that I can enact in others, so I didn’t regard a career until I learned more about it. Overall, I absolutely adore the computer science field and can't wait to go into it.
